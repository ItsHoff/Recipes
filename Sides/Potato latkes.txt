Potato latkes

3-4 Potatoes (shredded, best with skin left on)
Egg
Green onion (finely chopped)
1/2-1 tsp Garlic powder
Lightly salted butter
S
queeze excess moisture from shredded potatoes, and place potatoes into a mixing bowl. Begin heating a frying pan at medium setting. Break egg over shredded potatoes and mix well. Add finely chopped green onion and garlic powder and mix until evenly dispersed. Drop butter onto the pan to evenly coat it, and drop three tbsp of the mixture onto it. Flatten out quickly and fry on both sides until crispy.
