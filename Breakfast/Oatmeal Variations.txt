Oatmeal Variations

Base (for reference)
1 cup rolled oats
2 cups water
¼ tsp salt

coconut and lime oatmeal
¼ cup coconut, shredded
2 tbsp sugar
½ lime, juiced 

berry oatmeal
½ cup berries, fresh or frozen
1 tbsp sugar

pumpkin oatmeal
½ cup canned pumpkin
¾ cup milk (or almond / soy milk)
1¼ cups water
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
optional
¼ tsp ginger powder
¼ tsp clove powder
maple syrup

baklava oatmeal
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tbsp orange zest, finely grated
4 tbsp honey
2 tbsp almonds or pistachios, chopped

apple cinnamon oatmeal
2 cups apple juice or cider
1 tsp cinnamon
1 apple, cored and chopped
