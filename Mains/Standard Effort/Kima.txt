Kima

2lbs ground beef
4tbsp butter
1 onion, diced
5 cloves garlic, minced
Roughly 1 inch piece of ginger, minced
7ish oz of tomato paste
4 sweet potatoes, diced
2 tbsp curry powder
3tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp turmeric
1tsp black pepper
8oz spinach
handful of finely chopped cilantro

Directions:
1) Heat butter over medium heat. Add onion and saute until soft. Add ginger and garlic and saute another minute or so until fragrant.
2) Add beef and cook until browned
3) Add tomato paste and seasonings, stir well
4) Add sweet potato, turn heat down to med-low. Cover and cook for about 15 minutes or until sweet potato is cooked through. I usually give it a stir partway through.
5) Add spinach, cook covered until soft
6) Stir in chopped cilantro and serve
It's a super easy recipe, doesn't take long, and is a hit with everyone I've shared it with so far.
