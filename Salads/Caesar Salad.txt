Caesar Salad

2 to 3 romaine lettuce hearts 
or 1 pound kale
1/2 recipe Dressing
1 recipe Croutons
1 oz parmesan

Dressing
1 cup mayo
2 oz parmesan
2 tsp Worchesterschire sauce
4 anchovy fillets
2 cloves garlic
1/2 cup evoo

Croutons
3 tbsp evoo
1 clove garlic
1/2 loaf bread
1 oz parmesan
