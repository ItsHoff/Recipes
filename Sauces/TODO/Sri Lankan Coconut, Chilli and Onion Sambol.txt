Sri Lankan Coconut, Chilli and Onion Sambol
Author: Dan Toombs
Ingredients
Shredded meat from one coconut
3 red chilli peppers
Salt to taste
A slash of water
1 red onion
Juice of one or two limes
Instructions
1. Using a pestle and mortar or a grinding stone if you have one, start by grinding the chilli, salt and water to a paste.
2. Add the coconut and onion and keep grinding until you have a chunky, thick paste.
3. Add lime juice to taste and serve.
