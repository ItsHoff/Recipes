LIME BUTTER SAUCE

YIELD: Makes about 3/4 cup
ACTIVE TIME: 5 min
TOTAL TIME: 5 min
INGREDIENTS

1 large garlic clove, chopped
1/4 cup (n. 1.5 - 2) fresh lime juice
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
1 stick (1/2 cup) unsalted butter, melted
PREPARATION

Pur√©e garlic with lime juice, salt, and pepper in a blender until smooth. With motor running, add melted butter and blend until emulsified, about 30 seconds.

cooks' note:
Lime butter sauce can be made 1 day ahead and chilled, covered. Stir before using.
