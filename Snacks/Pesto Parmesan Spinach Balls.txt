Pesto Parmesan Spinach Balls

    Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Yield: 10 

Ingredients

    2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained very well
    4 eggs, beaten
    1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    1/2 cup prepared pesto
    1 small yellow onion, grated
    1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    1 1/2 â€“ 2 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs
    Marinara, to serve (optional)

Instructions

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
    In a medium bowl, all of the ingredients together, adding breadcrumbs as needed. Shape the mixture into 1 inch balls.
    Arrange the balls in a single layer on a large baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned and cooked through, about 15-20 minutes.
    Serve warm with marinara.
